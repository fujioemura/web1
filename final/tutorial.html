1.

Before Shooting...

Find out the time of the shoot.

Find out what camera will be used

Find the location that will be suitable with the concept that you decide.

Double check everything you need so the shooting day will go smoothly.

2.

While Shooting...

Figure out the direction of the light

As a photographer, it is important to keep in mind that the subject needs to feel comfortable in order to get the best result.

Direct the model to get the feeling that you want.

3.

After Shooting...

The selection process starts from elimination; Eliminate images that doesn't communicate strongly.

After selection, edit them in any photo-editing software including lightroom,Photoshop, Capture One etc.

Organize the images so you'll be able to easily find them when you need.
